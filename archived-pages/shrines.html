Shrines

Video Game

‘Shrines’ is an open-world collection game. After finding a mysterious shrine, you enter it and are teleported away to a strange realm. You decide to gather energy to try to activate the shrine again, hoping that it will take you back to where you came from. I made the first level, compiled the game (and made all levels consistent), and made the video.
