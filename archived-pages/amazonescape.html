Amazon Escape

Board Game

‘Amazon Escape’ is a survival board game. Players have survived a plane crash, located in the central Amazon region, and must navigate their way out to a Rescue Point, indicated on the map. If playing the Free-for-All game mode, each player is on their own to try to escape, whereas the Cooperative game mode requires all players to escape to really win.

As players explore the uncharted map, they will come across various geographical features that present them with different challenges and encounters. Players also have a Health meter, which can not be allowed to reach 0, and have a hand of cards, which they can use to move, scout, restore health, and do various other things.

The game consists of 30+ unique Encounter cards, 9 Resource cards, 7 Terrain types, and each game will play differently since the map is procedurally generated as you play.
