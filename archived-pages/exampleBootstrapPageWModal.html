Tower of Babel

Introduction

In this single-player Action Adventure RPG, the player grows in power as they ascend the mystical Tower of Babel. Hoping to bring about an end to the world's plagues and suffering, the player must defeat the monstrous and powerful creatures that have infested the tower as they make their way to its peak.

Features


• Climb the Tower's many Floors, each featuring distinct Landscapes and Monsters
• Fight Monsters and Dangerous Bosses in Turn-Based Combat
• Recruit and Train Companions
• Collect Weapons and Items with Powerful Abilities
• Learn about the World and its People by Completing Quests
• Find Hidden Treasures
