The Forest of Sharill

‘The Forest of Sharill’ is a text adventure game. It is about exploring the Forest and uncovering the some of the secrets and curious things within the forest. There is a way to beat the game (or will be at v1.0), but in order to figure it out, you'll probably have to play it many times to be able to understand and plan your playthrough well enough to be able to do all that you need to do in order to be able to get to the winning conditions.

Note: Currently, this game does have some incomplete gameplay, mainly with a few encounters. And, there may be a few bugs and/or typos. I apologize for them, but I've done the best I can to iron out them before releasing. I also will continue to iron out and improve the design and visual information so that it's all more comfortable, natural, and intuitive to process.
