The Forest of Sharill

‘The Forest of Sharill’ is a text adventure game. It is about exploring the Forest and uncovering the some of the secrets and curious things within the forest. There are a few endings, but in order to figure them out, you'll probably have to play it many times to be able to understand and plan your playthrough well enough to be able to do all that you need to do in order to be able to get to the winning conditions.
