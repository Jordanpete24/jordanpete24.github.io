Arctic Traveler

‘Arctic Traveler’ is a 2.5D exploration and platforming game. The goal is to travel through the various levels in shifting character forms to escape the Icy Cavern you start it to ultimately rise through the Stark Skies and ascend to the heavens as a bubble. Some levels have additional mechanics, such as upgrades or bonus items. And each level has different movement physics, as the form (and to some extent nature) of your character and the medium its in changes.
