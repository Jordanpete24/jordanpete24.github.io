Castle Siege

‘Castle Siege’ is a party card game. It is a 2-sided game where the Castle player is trying to defend itself against oncoming attacks from the Siege players. The cards that each player plays are used to determine how much damage the Castle receives. It can be played by 3-6 players, can be played in ~10-25 minutes and, aside from the instructions, all you need is a deck of cards, somewhere to play, and something to take notes with.
