Castle Siege

‘Castle Siege’ is a party card game. It is a 2-sided game when the Castle player is trying to defend itself against oncoming attacks from the Siege players. That cards that each player plays is used to determine how much damage the Castle receives. It can be played by 3-6 players, can be played in ~10-25 minutes and, aside from the instructions, all you need is a deck of cards and somewhere to take notes.
