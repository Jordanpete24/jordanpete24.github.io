Predatory Evolution

‘Predatory Evolution’ is a 3D on-rails shooter game. That game is about playing as various predatory creatures and, as you compete for your place in the food chain, evolving to become more and more powerful and dominant. My level, 'Long Lake,' involves a gull shooing away competition, eating fish, and avoiding obstacles to in order to evolve.
